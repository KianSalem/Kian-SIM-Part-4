Kian's SIM Part 4: Breaking Stereotypes - Beyond Sexual Objectification

This site is dedicated to dissecting the impact of media on our perceptions of sexuality, and shining a light on the sexual objectification that permeates popular culture. Despite being a Journalism major, I decided to go with the website form of media because I find it to not only be a useful skill but a therapeutic form of expression. If you hadn't already noticed, I made everything UT themed! Also this wasn't made in Wix or anything like that - I wrote all the code myself. So I apologize for the less-than-perfect formatting.

About This Project

My goal is to challenge and deconstruct the patterns of sexual objectification prevalent in media. I believe in promoting a respectful portrayal of sexuality and this website serves as a hub for awareness building.
