Welcome to Breaking Stereotypes: Beyond Sexual Objectification

This site is dedicated to dissecting the impact of media on our perceptions of sexuality, and shining a light on sexual objectification that permeates popular culture.

About This Project

Our goal is to challenge and deconstruct the patterns of sexual objectification prevalent in media. We believe in promoting a respectful, diverse, and balanced portrayal of sexuality, and this platform serves as a hub for discussion, analysis, and awareness building.
